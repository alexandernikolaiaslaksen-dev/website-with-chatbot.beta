Snowmobile Safari

Experience the thrill of riding snowmobiles through pristine winter landscapes. Join our guided safaris for an unforgettable adventure in the snow!

Pricing

  • Half-day safari: $150 per person
  • Full-day safari: $250 per person
  • Group discounts available for parties of 5 or more

Schedule

Our snowmobile safaris run every weekend during the winter season.

  • Saturdays: Departures at 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM
  • Sundays: Departures at 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM
  • Advance booking required. Check weather conditions before departure.
